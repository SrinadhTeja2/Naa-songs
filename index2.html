Aadi



Aadi is a 2002 Indian Telugu-language action film written and directed by V. V. Vinayak. The film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr., Keerthi Chawla, and Sayaji Shinde in lead roles, while Prakash Raj, Ali, and Brahmanandam play supporting roles. The film's music was composed by Devi Sri Prasad.
