Janatha Garage


Janatha Garage is a 2016 Indian Telugu-language action drama film written and directed by Koratala Siva. The film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr., Mohanlal, Samantha Ruth Prabhu, and Nithya Menen in lead roles, while Sai Kumar, Devayani, and Unni Mukundan play supporting roles. The film's music was composed by Devi Sri Prasad.

Teaser

The teaser of Janatha Garage was released on 15 July 2016. The film was released on 1 September 2016 and received positive reviews from critics and audience alike.
