Akhanda

Akhanda (transl. Whole/Undivided) is a 2021 Indian Telugu-language fantasy action drama film[7] co-written and directed by Boyapati Srinu. It was produced by Miryala Ravinder Reddy, under Dwaraka Creations. It stars Nandamuri Balakrishna in a dual role alongside Pragya Jaiswal, Jagapathi Babu and Srikanth. The film featured original score and soundtrack was composed by Thaman S. Released on 2 December 2021, Akhanda opened to mixed reviews from the critics and audience, alike.[8] However, the film became a massive commercial success, grossing over ₹121–150 crores worldwide, emerging as the second highest-grossing Telugu film of the year and the highest-grossing film in Balakrishna's career then.[9][c] Akhanda was featured at the 53rd International Film Festival of India in the Indian Panorama mainstream section.[12]
