Jai Lava Kusa


Jai Lava Kusa is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language action drama film written and directed by K. S. Ravindra. The film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr. in a triple role, alongside Raashi Khanna and Nivetha Thomas in lead roles, while Ronit Roy plays the antagonist.
