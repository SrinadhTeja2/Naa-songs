Bhagavanth Kesari

Bhagavanth Kesari is a 2023 Indian Telugu-language action drama film directed by Anil Ravipudi. The film stars N. B.K in the lead role, alongside Kajal Aggarwal and Arjun Rampal in pivotal roles. The film's music was composed by Thaman S.
