Temper

Temper is a 2015 Indian Telugu-language action drama film directed by Puri Jagannadh. The film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr. in the lead role, alongside Kajal Aggarwal and Prakash Raj in pivotal roles. The film's music was composed by Anup Rubens.
